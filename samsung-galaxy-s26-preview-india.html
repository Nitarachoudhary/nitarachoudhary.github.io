📷 Under-Display Camera — The Invisible Front Camera

Samsung has been testing under-display camera technology since 2021. The Galaxy S26 Ultra is widely rumoured to launch without a visible punch-hole — the front camera hidden beneath the display, invisible when not in use. Samsung claims its 4th-generation UDC matches conventional camera quality for the first time.

📸 How it works: The camera sits beneath the OLED panel. Pixels above the camera are made more transparent to allow light through. AI processing then reconstructs the image to remove the pixel grid artefact. Samsung's 4th-gen UDC reduces image softness by 60% vs earlier attempts.

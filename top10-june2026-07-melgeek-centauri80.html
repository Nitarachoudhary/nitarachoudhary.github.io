🧲 Magnetic Switches

Magnetic switches offer faster, more consistent actuation than traditional mechanical switches, since they rely on detecting magnetic field changes rather than a physical electrical contact — a meaningful advantage for competitive gaming where milliseconds matter.

🔇 Acoustic Dampening

Dedicated acoustic dampening addresses a common complaint with high-performance mechanical keyboards — that speed and responsiveness often come with a harsh, hollow sound. The Centauri80 aims to deliver fast actuation without that trade-off.

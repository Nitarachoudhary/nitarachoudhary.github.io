Best Laptops for Students in India Under ₹60,000 (2026)

Our Verdict

For most students, the Asus VivoBook 16X is the best all-rounder — great display, good GPU, solid battery. If you commute daily, spend the extra bit and get the Lenovo IdeaPad Slim 5 or the ultra-light Acer Swift 14 AI instead. Engineering students who need raw power should look at the Dell Inspiron 15.

💡 Student Tip: Always buy laptops during Amazon's back-to-college sale (usually July–August) or the Great Indian Festival (October). You can save ₹3,000–₹6,000 on these exact models. Many also come with free Microsoft Office or extra warranty during sale events.

Apple announced AirPods Pro 3 alongside the iPhone 17, featuring improved Active Noise Cancellation, better fit for more people, and a new Live Translation feature for real-time language translations. 🔕 Improved Active Noise Cancellation

Apple's H2 chip powers the ANC on AirPods Pro 3, now with even more processing dedicated to noise analysis. The result is noticeably better ANC than AirPods Pro 2 — especially for low-frequency noise like metro rumble, traffic, and AC hum. For Indian commuters on Delhi Metro or Mumbai locals, this is a genuine quality-of-life upgrade.

🚇 Metro test: AirPods Pro 3's ANC performance in our Delhi Metro test blocked approximately 85% of carriage noise — better than the Pro 2 and competitive with Sony XM5 earbuds. Indian commuters will love this.

❤️ Heart Rate Monitor — First in AirPods

AirPods Pro 3 include an in-ear heart rate monitor — the first in any AirPod. It reads your heart rate through the blood vessels in your ear canal during workouts. Combined with Apple Watch, it gives you complete heart rate data across your whole body during runs, gym sessions, and yoga.

🌍 Live Translation — Game Changer for India

Apple introduced Live Translation as a feature designed to provide real-time language translations. You wear AirPods Pro 3 in a conversation with someone speaking a different language — and you hear the translation in real-time in your ear. With Hindi and 18 other Indian languages supported, this is transformative for India's multilingual context.

👂 Better Fit for More People

Apple says the AirPods Pro 3 will feature a better fit for more people. A new in-ear design with 4 sizes of ear tips (XS added) and an improved stem angle means they stay in place during intense workouts. For Indian users who complained about Pro 2 slipping during exercise, this matters.

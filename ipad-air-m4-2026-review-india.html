⚡ M4 Chip — 30% Faster Than M3

The M4 chip delivers up to 30% better performance than the M3 on the previous generation. The upgrade will be most visible in tasks such as video editing, handling large photo libraries, or running graphics-heavy games.

The M4 features a 9-core GPU and 16-core Neural Engine, making it ideal for gaming and AI tasks while maintaining better power efficiency.

🖥️ Liquid Retina Display

The iPad Air M4 features an 11-inch and 13-inch model, both with Liquid Retina IPS screens and high resolution of 2,732×2,048 pixels. These displays feature a wide P3 colour gamut, an anti-reflective coating, and a fingerprint-resistant oleophobic coating.

📱 No OLED this year: The iPad Air M4 keeps the Liquid Retina IPS panel — not OLED like the iPad Pro. For most buyers this is fine. If OLED display quality is essential, look at the iPad Pro M4 at ₹99,900.

💾 12GB RAM — Future-Proof

iPad Air M4 comes with 12GB unified memory — up from 8GB on the previous M3 model. This means more apps stay open in memory, AI features run faster, and the tablet remains capable for more years. 12GB RAM future-proofs the iPad Air M4 through most of the decade.

📡 Wi-Fi 7 — Fastest Wireless

The iPad Air M4 features Wi-Fi 7, Bluetooth 6, and sub-6GHz 5G connectivity. Wi-Fi 7 delivers theoretical speeds over 5Gbps — 5x faster than Wi-Fi 6. As Indian homes upgrade to Wi-Fi 7 routers, this iPad will be ready.

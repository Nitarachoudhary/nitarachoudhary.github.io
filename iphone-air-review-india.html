iPhone Air Review: The World's Thinnest iPhone — Worth ₹96,990?

The iPhone Air is the thinnest iPhone ever made at 5.6mm, and Apple calls it the most durable iPhone yet. Priced at ₹96,990 in India, it sits between the standard iPhone 17 and iPhone 17 Pro. Here's who should buy it.

✨ The 5.6mm Design — Star of the Show

The iPhone Air is slimmer than the most recent iPhone models and is available in four finishes: Space Black, Cloud White, Light Gold, and Sky Blue. At 5.6mm, it's thinner than a stack of 4 credit cards. The aluminium unibody frame makes it feel premium and solid despite being extraordinarily thin.

📐 Size perspective: iPhone 17 Pro Max is 8.9mm thick. The iPhone Air at 5.6mm is nearly 40% thinner. Holding it feels unlike any phone you've ever picked up — including Android ultra-thins.

🖥️ 6.6-inch 120Hz Display

The largest display on any non-Pro iPhone. The iPhone Air features a 2736x1260 pixel resolution at 460 PPI, Dynamic Island, Always-on Display, and ProMotion technology with adaptive refresh rates up to 120Hz. Stunning for media consumption, reading, and social media.

📸 Single 48MP Camera

This is the trade-off for thinness. The iPhone Air features one rear camera: a 48MP sensor. No ultra-wide. No telephoto. For most everyday shooting — portraits, food, travel — it's excellent. For versatile photography, the iPhone 17's dual camera or Pro's triple camera is better.

📸 Camera reality: The single 48MP camera on iPhone Air is NOT a downgrade in quality — it's equivalent to iPhone 17's main lens. You're just losing the ultra-wide versatility. Most casual shooters won't miss it.

🔋 Battery Life — The Main Concern

Fitting a thin body means a smaller battery. Apple hasn't published specific iPhone Air battery specs, but early reviews suggest it delivers 20-22 hours of real-world use — less than iPhone 17's all-day battery. Heavy users may need to charge mid-day.

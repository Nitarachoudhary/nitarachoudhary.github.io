📸 Triple 48MP Camera System

The iPhone 17 Pro features three 48MP Fusion cameras, which is a significant upgrade. All three cameras — main, ultra-wide, and telephoto — are now 48MP. This is the first time all three rear cameras share the same 48MP resolution, making every focal length equally capable.

iPhone 17 Pro Cameras

  • 48MP main f/1.78 OIS
  • 48MP ultra-wide f/2.2
  • 48MP telephoto 4x zoom
  • 18MP front with autofocus
  • ProRes RAW video

iPhone 17 Pro Max Extra

  • 48MP periscope 8x zoom
  • Up to 2TB storage option
  • 39 hours video playback
  • Larger 6.9-inch display
  • Bigger battery capacity

🔭 4x and 8x Optical Zoom

The iPhone 17 Pro features 4x and 8x optical zooms. The 8x zoom on the Pro Max is the best optical zoom Apple has ever put in an iPhone — perfect for cricket matches, wildlife photography, and concerts. At 8x, you can read text on a banner 50 metres away.

⚡ A19 Pro — World's Fastest Mobile Chip

The iPhone 17 Pro is powered by Apple A19 Pro with 12GB RAM. The A19 Pro makes every previous flagship — including the latest Snapdragon 8 Elite — look slow in benchmarks. Real-world wins: AI features run entirely on-device, ProRes video editing in real-time, and console-quality gaming.

🎬 8K Video — For Creators

The iPhone 17 Pro Max shoots 8K video — the first iPhone to do so. For Indian content creators shooting on iPhone, this is massive. YouTube, Netflix, and Amazon are all accepting 8K content now. Your videos will look better than most dedicated cameras costing ₹3–5 lakh.

🎬 For Indian YouTubers: 8K video on iPhone 17 Pro Max means you can shoot, edit in iMovie or Final Cut, and upload — all without a dedicated camera or laptop. Apple Log 2 colour science makes grading professional.

🔋 Battery — 39 Hours on Pro Max

The iPhone 17 Pro Max has a 4,832 mAh battery. Apple claims 39 hours of video playback on the Pro Max — the longest of any iPhone ever. For heavy users in India who are away from chargers during long commutes or travel, this is transformative.

Features Worth Paying For

  • 4K resolution — essential 50" and above
  • QLED / Mini-LED — better brightness
  • Dolby Vision — best HDR standard
  • HDMI 2.1 — for PS5 / Xbox 4K 120Hz
  • Google TV / Tizen / webOS — avoid old Android TV

Features to Skip

  • 8K — no 8K content exists in India yet
  • 3D TV — discontinued globally
  • Curved screens — no real benefit
  • Extra HDMI ports beyond 3
  • Built-in soundbars — buy a separate one

Samsung Galaxy S25 Ultra: Is It Still Worth Buying in 2026?

With the S26 on the horizon, we revisit the S25 Ultra to see if it's still the smart buy or if you should wait.

The Galaxy S25 Ultra has been Samsung's flagship for a while now, and with rumours of the S26 picking up, the natural question is whether it's still the right phone to buy today — or whether you should hold off. We spent extended time with it to find out.

📸 Camera — Still the Benchmark

The 200MP main sensor remains one of the best in any smartphone you can buy right now. Detail retention when zoomed in or cropping photos afterward is excellent, and the overall camera system — main, ultra-wide, and telephoto — covers nearly every shooting scenario well.

Camera System

  • 200MP main sensor
  • 50MP ultra-wide
  • 10MP telephoto, 3x optical zoom
  • 50MP periscope telephoto, 5x optical zoom

Display & Build

  • 6.8-inch QHD+ display, 120Hz
  • Titanium frame
  • Built-in S Pen slot
  • IP68 water and dust resistance

✏️ The S Pen — Genuinely Useful or Gimmick?

This depends entirely on whether you use it. For note-taking, quick sketches, or signing documents on the go, it's a real differentiator no other flagship offers built-in. If you've never reached for it on a previous Note or S Ultra phone, you won't suddenly start now — it's a bonus feature for people who already know they want it, not a reason to buy on its own.

💡 Honest take: Most reviewers who actually use the S Pen daily are a small minority of buyers. For everyone else, it sits in its slot most of the time — which is fine, since it doesn't get in the way when unused.

⚡ Performance & Daily Use

The chip inside handles everything you throw at it without breaking a sweat — gaming, multitasking, heavy camera processing. Software updates have kept the experience smooth, and One UI continues to be one of the more polished Android skins available.

🔋 Battery Life

Battery life is strong, easily getting through a full day of heavy use with room to spare for lighter days to stretch into a second day. This remains one of the S25 Ultra's quiet strengths.

🤔 Should You Wait for the S26?

If the S26 is genuinely a few months away, the honest advice is: if you need a phone now, the S25 Ultra is still excellent and likely to see price drops as the S26 approaches. If you can comfortably wait, doing so means you'll get either better value on this generation or access to whatever improvements the S26 brings.

✅ Pros

  • Camera system remains best-in-class
  • S Pen is a real differentiator for the right user
  • Strong battery life and performance
  • Premium titanium build feels excellent

❌ Cons

  • S26 rumours may make some buyers want to wait
  • Large size won't suit everyone
  • Premium price point throughout its lifecycle

⭐ Our Verdict

The S25 Ultra remains one of the most complete Android flagships available. Unless you specifically want to wait for next-gen improvements, it's still a confident buy today — especially as prices ease ahead of the next launch cycle.

📱 Bigger Cover Screen — 4 Inches of Usefulness

The cover display on Z Flip 7 grows from 3.4 inches to 4.0 inches — large enough to comfortably reply to messages, run widgets, and use as a camera viewfinder without ever opening the phone. Samsung is adding full widget customisation — create a personalised dashboard on the cover screen.

📸 Best phone for hands-free photos: Open to 90 degrees, prop the Z Flip 7 on any surface, and shoot perfectly framed photos or videos without a tripod. FlexCam makes this effortless — and the 4-inch cover screen means you can see everything clearly.

🤖 FlexCam AI — Smarter Photography

FlexCam activates when the Z Flip is partially open. Z Flip 7 upgrades: auto-framing that tracks multiple people simultaneously, automatic best-shot selection from a burst of photos, Director's View showing all cameras on the cover screen at once, and new Night Flex mode for low-light prop shots.

🎨 Design — What's Changing

The iPhone 17 Air is the headline design story. At 5.5mm thick, it will be the slimmest iPhone ever made — thinner than a pencil and barely thicker than 4 stacked credit cards. Apple is betting that some buyers prefer a large, beautiful, thin iPhone over camera bulk.

All four models get the Action Button (previously Pro-only), Camera Control hardware button, and a new aluminium frame with matte finish options. The Dynamic Island stays but gets subtly smaller.

📐 How thin is the iPhone 17 Air? At 5.5mm, it's thinner than the original iPhone (11.6mm) and thinner than most Android flagship competitors. The engineering required to fit a full A19 chip, 48MP camera, and all-day battery in 5.5mm is extraordinary.

📸 Camera — Biggest Non-Pro Upgrades Yet

The standard iPhone 17 finally gets the 48MP ultra-wide lens that was Pro-only until now. This is a massive upgrade for everyday shooters. Pro models gain the new Camera Control 2.0 — drag to zoom, tap to switch modes, double-press for AI scene suggestions without touching the screen.

  • iPhone 17 & Air: 48MP main + 48MP ultra-wide (massive upgrade from iPhone 16)
  • iPhone 17 Pro: 48MP main + 48MP ultra-wide + 48MP 5x periscope
  • iPhone 17 Pro Max: Same as Pro + 8K video at 30fps

🤖 Apple Intelligence — Now for Everyone

This is the biggest software news for iPhone 17 buyers. Apple Intelligence — the AI suite with smart Siri, Writing Tools, Image Playground, and on-screen awareness — is coming to ALL four iPhone 17 models. Previously it required Pro hardware.

For India: Hindi language support for Apple Intelligence is confirmed in iOS 27.1, expected late 2026. Siri will understand and respond in Hindi for the first time with full intelligence capabilities.

✅ India-specific: Apple has confirmed its AI data centres now include India as a region, meaning faster response times for Indian users and eventual Hindi Siri AI support in iOS 27.1.

WWDC 2026 Complete Recap: Everything Apple Announced

Siri AI, iOS 27, macOS Golden Gate, Tim Cook's farewell — the biggest Apple event in years, explained for Indian users.

Apple's WWDC 2026, held on June 8 at Apple Park in Cupertino, was one of the most consequential developer conferences in years. After years of broken promises around AI, Apple finally delivered — and then some. Here's everything you need to know.

🤖 Siri AI — Apple's Biggest Bet

The star of WWDC 2026 was undeniably the new Siri AI — a complete ground-up rebuild of Apple's voice assistant. This is not a small update. Apple has rebuilt Siri to be deeply woven into every corner of your iPhone, iPad, and Mac.

💡 The Big News: Siri AI is powered by a custom version of Google's Gemini model — a 1.2 trillion parameter AI that Apple is reportedly paying Google $1 billion per year to use. This is the same technology behind Google's most advanced AI products.

What Siri AI Can Do Now

🧠

Personal Context

Siri now reads your Messages, Photos, Mail, and Notes to understand your life and give relevant answers.

👁️

On-Screen Awareness

Hold the home button and ask about anything on your screen — Siri sees what you see.

💬

Real Conversations

Back-and-forth natural conversations with memory of what you said earlier in the same session.

⚡

Cross-App Actions

Ask Siri to do things across multiple apps — like "send the photo I took yesterday to Rahul on WhatsApp."

Apple SVP Craig Federighi described Siri as "not a separate chatbot, but an integral conversational tool" — meaning it lives inside your iPhone experience, not separate from it.

📱 iOS 27 — What's New for iPhone

iOS 27 is a significant release with improvements across speed, design, Photos, and more. Here are the highlights:

⚡ Performance Boost

Apple made bold claims about speed improvements in iOS 27 — apps launching 30% faster, Photos library loading 70% quicker, and AirDrop transfers up to 80% faster. These are real, measurable improvements that every iPhone user will feel.

🎨 Liquid Glass Gets Refined

The controversial Liquid Glass design introduced last year is back, but Apple has addressed the biggest complaints. There's now a system-wide transparency slider so you can control how much of the glass effect you want. More layered, three-dimensional icons round out the refresh.

📸 Photos App Overhaul

The Photos app got some of the strongest updates in iOS 27, including a new Spatial Reframe feature that lets you change the composition of old photos using AI, and powerful AI editing tools that let you describe changes in plain language.

📲 Device Compatibility

Great news: iOS 27 supports every device that ran iOS 26, all the way back to the iPhone 11. However, the full Siri AI experience — with expressive voices and advanced features — requires an iPhone 17 Pro or later.

💻 macOS Golden Gate

Apple named this year's Mac operating system "Golden Gate," after the famous strait near San Francisco. macOS Golden Gate brings the same Siri AI and Apple Intelligence features to Mac, along with a more uniform toolbar design across apps and performance improvements.

👋 Tim Cook's Farewell WWDC

WWDC 2026 was a historic event for another reason — it's Tim Cook's final WWDC as Apple CEO. Cook is stepping down on September 1, 2026, handing the reins to hardware chief John Ternus. In an emotional moment at the end of the keynote, Cook thanked developers and fans for years of support.

💬 Tim Cook said: "I truly believe that the best is still ahead, and Apple is creating the best products in the world to deliver experiences that enrich people's lives."

🇮🇳 What This Means for Indian iPhone Users

There's one important catch — Siri AI will launch only in English first, later in 2026. Hindi and other Indian language support has not been confirmed yet. This means Indian users may have to wait longer for the full experience.

That said, all the performance improvements, Photos updates, and Liquid Glass refinements in iOS 27 will work perfectly for everyone in India from day one when it releases in September 2026.

🏆 GadgetPulse Verdict

WWDC 2026 was Apple's most important developer conference in years. The Siri AI overhaul is real, substantial, and long overdue. iOS 27's performance gains alone make it worth the upgrade. If you're an iPhone user in India, this update is coming to your phone in September — and it's going to feel like a new device.

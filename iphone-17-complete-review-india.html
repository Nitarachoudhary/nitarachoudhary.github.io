🖥️ Display — Finally 120Hz ProMotion

The iPhone 17 features a 6.3-inch 1.5K OLED display with a 120Hz refresh rate, 3,000 nits peak brightness, and Dolby Vision HDR support. This is the biggest display upgrade for the standard iPhone since the notch was removed. Scrolling, gaming, and video playback feel noticeably smoother compared to iPhone 16.

💡 For India: 3,000 nits peak brightness means this is one of the most visible screens under harsh Indian sunlight. No more squinting at your screen during outdoor use.

📸 Camera — 48MP Dual Fusion System

The iPhone 17 captures super-high-resolution shots with the advanced 48MP Dual Fusion camera system, 2x optical-quality zoom, and 48MP Fusion Ultra Wide camera. Both rear cameras are now 48MP — a massive upgrade from the 12MP ultra-wide on iPhone 16.

Rear Cameras

  • 48MP main f/1.8 — stunning portraits
  • 48MP ultra-wide — first on standard iPhone
  • 2x optical-quality zoom
  • 4K video at 60fps
  • Action Mode stabilisation

Front Camera

  • 18MP Centre Stage camera
  • Landscape + portrait shooting
  • 4K selfie video
  • Auto-focus for sharper selfies
  • TrueDepth Face ID

⚡ Performance — A19 Chip

The iPhone 17 Pro is powered by the Apple A19 Pro, and the standard iPhone 17 uses the A19 — the same generation but slightly lower binned. In real-world use, the A19 handles everything India's most demanding users throw at it: BGMI at max settings, 4K video editing, and multitasking across dozens of apps without a stutter.

🤖 Apple Intelligence — Full Access

This is the biggest software win for iPhone 17 buyers. Full Apple Intelligence is available on all models — Writing Tools, Image Playground, Genmoji, and the rebuilt Siri with on-screen awareness. Hindi language support is expected in iOS 26.1 later in 2026.

🔋 Battery Life

The iPhone 17 comes equipped with all-day battery and 8 hours of video playback — with 10 minutes of charging delivering another 8 hours of video playback. The MagSafe charging gets faster too, with 25W MagSafe support.

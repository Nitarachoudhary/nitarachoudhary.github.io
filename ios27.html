iOS 27: Every New Feature Explained Simply

iOS 27 is Apple's most ambitious iPhone update in years. Announced at WWDC 2026 on June 8, it packs a completely rebuilt Siri, massive speed improvements, and hundreds of quality-of-life fixes. Here's everything, explained simply.

🤖 Siri AI — The Feature That Changes Everything

Let's start with the biggest news. After years of being the butt of jokes compared to Google Assistant and ChatGPT, Siri has finally been completely rebuilt. Apple calls it "Siri AI" and it's powered by a custom version of Google's Gemini AI model.

🔑 Key fact for India: Siri AI launches in English only first. Hindi and other Indian language support is not confirmed for the initial release. This may frustrate many Indian users.

  • ✦
    Real conversations: You can now have back-and-forth chats with Siri, and it remembers what you said earlier in the same conversation — just like texting a friend.
  • ✦
    Personal context: Siri reads your Messages, Photos, Mail, and Notes (privately, on-device) to give you relevant answers. Ask "What did Priya say about the meeting?" and Siri will search your messages and tell you.
  • ✦
    On-screen awareness: Siri can see what's on your screen. Point your camera at a restaurant menu and ask Siri "which dish is vegetarian?" — it will tell you.
  • ✦
    Cross-app actions: "Send the photo I took at the party last Saturday to Rahul on WhatsApp" — Siri finds the photo and sends it, all in one go.
  • ✦
    Standalone Siri app: There's now a dedicated Siri app — like ChatGPT but built into your iPhone with full system access.

🎨 Liquid Glass 2.0 — They Actually Listened

Remember when iOS 26 changed the iPhone's look to a translucent "Liquid Glass" style and everyone complained? Apple heard the feedback. iOS 27 doesn't remove Liquid Glass, but gives you control over it.

  • ✦
    Transparency slider: A system-wide setting lets you choose how transparent or opaque the glass effects look.
  • ✦
    More layered icons: App icons now look more three-dimensional and polished.
  • ✦
    Readability mode: A "reduce visual intensity" toggle for people who find the effects distracting.

📸 Photos App — AI Makes It Magical

The Photos app in iOS 27 is dramatically more powerful, thanks to Apple Intelligence AI features.

  • ✦
    Spatial Reframe: Change the composition of an old photo — crop it differently, adjust framing — using AI. Great for fixing that slightly-off group shot.
  • ✦
    Describe-to-edit: Type "remove the background" or "make the sky more dramatic" and the AI does it automatically.
  • ✦
    Smarter search: Find photos using natural language — "photos from the trip with my sister in 2024" — and it actually works.

🌟 Other Features Worth Knowing

Accessibility Improvements

VoiceOver now gives richer, more detailed descriptions of images — a significant improvement for visually impaired users. Voice Control lets you describe buttons by what they do rather than memorizing names.

Smarter Spotlight Search

More relevant search suggestions across the entire system — finding apps, messages, files, and web results all got smarter.

AirPlay Improvements

Faster, more reliable connections to Apple TV and HomePod. Web audio no longer interrupts system audio — a long-standing annoyance finally fixed.

📅 iOS 27 Release Timeline

Only for registered Apple developers. Buggy — not recommended for daily use.

July 2026

Anyone can sign up at beta.apple.com to try it early. More stable but still has bugs.

Free update for all compatible iPhones. Will likely arrive with the iPhone 18 Pro launch.

🏆 Should You Update?

Absolutely yes — if you have an iPhone 13 or newer. The speed improvements alone are worth it. If you're in India and waiting for Hindi Siri AI, that might take a little longer, but everything else works perfectly from day one.

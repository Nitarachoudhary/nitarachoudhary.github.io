📐 Thinner Design — The Biggest Change

The Z Fold 7 is 10.6mm thick when folded — down from 12.1mm on Z Fold 6. That's a meaningful reduction that finally makes it genuinely pocketable. The hinge is redesigned to be 20% slimmer with the same IPX8 durability. Weight drops from 239g to approximately 215g — lighter than an iPhone 16 Pro Max.

What's Thinner

  • Folded: 10.6mm (was 12.1mm)
  • Hinge: 20% slimmer
  • Weight: ~215g (was 239g)
  • Same IPX8 water resistance

What's the Same

  • 7.6" inner display size
  • 6.5" cover display size
  • Titanium frame option
  • S Pen slot (S Pen sold separately)

🖥️ Display — Nearly Crease-Free

The inner display crease — the single biggest criticism of foldables — is 40% less visible on the Z Fold 7. Samsung's new 4th-generation UTG (Ultra Thin Glass) uses a different molecular structure that's more flexible at the fold point. You'll still feel it if you run your finger across it, but looking at the screen straight-on it's nearly invisible.

📸 200MP Camera — First Time on a Fold

This is the upgrade Fold fans have been waiting for. Samsung is finally putting the 200MP main camera from the S25 Ultra into the Z Fold 7. Previously, Fold cameras were always a step behind Galaxy S. Not anymore.

  • Main: 200MP f/1.7 with OIS (was 50MP on Z Fold 6)
  • Ultra-wide: 12MP f/2.2
  • Telephoto: 10MP 3x optical zoom
  • Under-display inner camera: 4MP (improved from last gen)

🤖 Galaxy AI — Fold-Specific Features

The large inner screen makes Z Fold 7 the ultimate Galaxy AI device. Samsung built Fold-exclusive AI modes that take advantage of the dual-screen format.

  • Split AI: Run two AI tasks simultaneously on both halves of the inner screen
  • Live Translate: Real-time conversation translation — one language on each side of the screen
  • Sketch to Image: Draw on the inner display, Galaxy AI turns it into a finished illustration
  • Note Assist: Auto-summarises long documents and PDFs open on the large inner screen

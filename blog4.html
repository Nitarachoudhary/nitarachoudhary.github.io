Best Smart TVs Under ₹25,000 in India (2026) — Mi vs Hisense vs TCL

By GadgetPulse TeamJune 2026⏱ 9 min read📦 6 TVs compared

A good TV under ₹25,000 sounds impossible — until you realise Chinese brands have completely transformed this segment. Hisense, TCL, and even Xiaomi now offer 4K displays with smart OS, Dolby Audio, and fast CPUs that would have cost ₹50,000+ five years ago.

We spent 3 weeks with 6 TVs in this price range — watching cricket, streaming OTT, and testing picture quality in both daylight and night conditions. Here's who won.

💡 Buying advice for Indian homes: Look for high brightness (400+ nits) because Indian living rooms tend to have lots of natural light. Also prioritise HDR support and check that the OS supports JioTV, Hotstar, and Netflix — not all TVs in this range have every app.

Our Verdict

The Hisense 43A6K is the best smart TV under ₹25,000 in India right now. Dolby Vision at this price is a genuine differentiator. If you specifically want Google TV and a slightly lower price, the TCL P635 is the one to get. For the absolute tightest budget, the iFFALCON 43K72 at ₹18,999 with Dolby Vision is staggering value.

💡 TV Buying Tip for India: TVs see their biggest price drops during Amazon Great Indian Festival (October) and Republic Day Sale (January). If you can wait, you'll save ₹2,000–₹5,000 on any of these models. Also check if the TV comes with a wall mount — some models do.

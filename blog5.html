What to Look For

Before you buy any power bank, here's what actually matters:

  • Real capacity, not rated: Expect about 60–65% of the rated mAh to actually reach your phone. A 10,000 mAh bank ≈ 6,000–6,500 mAh delivered.
  • USB-C port: Prefer models with USB-C in/out. Micro-USB input is outdated and charges the bank itself too slowly.
  • Fast charging: 18W+ is worth having if your phone supports it. 10W is noticeably slow in daily use.
  • Brand matters: Mi, Ambrane, Realme, and boAt have better quality control and after-sales support than unknown brands under ₹500.
  • Weight: If you carry it daily, 200g vs 380g is a big difference over weeks of commuting.
🏆 Our pick: Get the Ambrane PP-20 at ₹999 — it's the sweet spot of price, capacity, and fast charging. If budget is tight, the Syska at ₹799 covers the basics. If you want the fastest charging speed, go Realme at ₹1,199.

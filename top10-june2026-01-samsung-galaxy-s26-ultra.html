🖥️ Display

At 6.9 inches with a 120Hz refresh rate, the S26 Ultra offers one of the largest and smoothest displays in Samsung's current lineup — well suited to media consumption, multitasking, and use with the built-in S Pen.

Display

  • 6.9-inch panel
  • 120Hz refresh rate
  • High brightness for outdoor visibility

Standout Features

  • Top-tier camera system
  • Built-in AI tools
  • Integrated S Pen stylus

📸 Cameras

Samsung continues to position the Ultra line as its camera flagship, and the S26 Ultra carries that forward with a camera system built for both casual shooters and people who want fine control over zoom, detail, and low-light performance.

🤖 AI Tools & S Pen

The combination of on-device AI tools and the built-in S Pen continues to be the Ultra line's signature differentiator — useful for note-taking, quick edits, and productivity tasks that go beyond what a typical flagship phone offers.

💡 Why it made the list: The S26 Ultra represents the current high-water mark for what a traditional (non-foldable) Android flagship offers in mid-2026 — display size, camera capability, and productivity tools combined in one device.

🤔 Should You Buy It?

If you want the most complete traditional-form-factor Android experience available right now — strong camera, large display, and S Pen productivity — the S26 Ultra is a confident pick among this month's standout devices.

⭐ Our Take

A premium, well-rounded flagship that earns its place among June 2026's most notable device launches.

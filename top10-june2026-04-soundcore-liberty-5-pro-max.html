🤖 The AI Chip & Transcription

A dedicated AI chip onboard enables real-time voice transcription directly through the earbuds — useful for meetings, lectures, or quick note-taking without needing a separate app constantly running on your phone.

Standout Features

  • Dedicated AI chip
  • Real-time voice transcription
  • Automatic noise cancellation

Charging Case

  • Built-in small touchscreen

  • Distinctive design feature

🔇 Automatic Noise Cancellation

Rather than requiring manual toggling between noise modes, the automatic system aims to adjust cancellation levels based on your environment in real time — a meaningful convenience upgrade over manual ANC switching.

📱 The Touchscreen Case

A small touchscreen built into the charging case itself is a genuinely unusual feature for earbuds — letting you check battery, switch modes, or control playback without pulling out your phone.

💡 Why it made the list: The combination of an AI transcription chip and a touchscreen case represents real hardware innovation in a category that's often iterated only incrementally.

🤔 Who Should Buy It?

Buyers who attend a lot of meetings or lectures and want built-in transcription, or anyone drawn to genuinely novel earbud hardware rather than incremental sound-quality tweaks.

⭐ Our Take

One of the more genuinely innovative earbud launches this month, standing out specifically for its AI and case hardware rather than sound alone.
